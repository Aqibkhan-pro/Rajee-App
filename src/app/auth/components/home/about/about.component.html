Protection from Human Exploitation Human Trafficking, Organ Trafficking

At Rajee, we work with the Human Rights Commission in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (hrc.gov.sa) to combat human trafficking, protect the rights of migrant workers, and provide necessary technical, preventative, and awareness-raising resources, as well as assistance to victims of human trafficking.

We do not tolerate the exploitation of our platform for any purposes that violate local and international human rights laws. The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia prohibits all forms of human trafficking, as defined in the Palermo Protocol, in accordance with international and regional standards.

Zero tolerance

When we detect content that violates human rights laws, we may refer it to the relevant authorities and suspend the offending userโs account.

How to Report Inappropriate Content

  1. Report the published ad from within the ad page.
  2. Report via "Contact Us".
  3. Report and contact the Human Rights Commission via email at Info@ncct.gov.sa.

Protection from Harassment or Abuse Racism, Hate, Threats

The laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia mandate that all state institutions treat all people fairly, regardless of their religion, race, gender, or nationality. At Rajee, we do not permit any user to engage in discrimination, threats, abuse, harm, or bullying, whether in published content or through private messages.

If you experience any abuse

  • Submit a report via "Contact Us".
  • File a complaint against the member with the security authorities.

Child Protection Guidance aligned with KSA Child Protection Law

At Rajee, we work with relevant authorities to guide content publishers in adhering to the Child Protection Law. The law considers harassment, exploitation, bullying, offensive language, and discrimination against children violations of their rights.

The law also prohibits production, publication, distribution, or possession of any material directed at children that glorifies behavior contrary to Islamic law, public order, or public morals.

Important

Users must ensure advertisements are free from content that could harm children. If violating content is detected, Rajee will take necessary action.

Report & contact authorities

  • Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development: hrsd.gov.sa
  • Human Rights Commission: hrc.gov.sa

Protecting Girls and Women Safety steps and platform tools

Girls and women have the right to feel safe using internet technologies for commerce, shopping, and communication. Rajee provides technical services that help safeguard users, such as blocking members and preventing them from sending private messages.

To protect yourself from harassment

  1. Do not send any private or personal content via messages.
  2. Do not share your phone number or contact others outside Rajee.
  3. If you feel unsafe, block the member via the block icon in messages.
  4. If you are being harassed, report the member.
  5. Contact us via "Contact Us".
  6. File a complaint with security authorities if needed.

Fraud Protection How we reduce scams + how you can stay safe

Rajee strives to create a safe and fraud-free environment by preventing link sharing in ads/replies/messages, warning users against communicating outside the platform, and providing reporting tools across ads, chats, and ratings.

Ways to Avoid Fraud

  • Search the other partyโs number in the โSuspended Numbersโ list on Rajee.
  • Do transactions face-to-face in a public, safe place.
  • Use โRajee Escrowโ if the transaction is not face-to-face.
  • Do not advertise a product you do not own.
  • Do not market job/commission offers that could be scams.
  • Never open links sent outside Rajee.
  • Do not share personal data.
  • Never share verification codes or passwords.

Ways to Report Fraud

  1. Report via "Contact Us".
  2. Go to the nearest police station and file a complaint.
